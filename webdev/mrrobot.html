Fun Fact about Penguins

Did you know that penguins are one of the most entertaining animals? Despite being birds, they can’t fly, but they sure know how to swim! Penguins waddle adorably on land, and they have an impressive sense of community. These creatures are found mostly in the Southern Hemisphere, and they often huddle together to stay warm in cold environments. Next time you feel chilly, just remember—you can always take a cue from a penguin!
